00029 Description
00030     Solver for unsteady 3D turbulent flow of perfect gas governed by
00031     quasi-gas dynamic (QGD) equations at high Mach numbers (from 2 to
00032     infinity).
00033 
00034     QGD system of equations has been developed by scientific group from
00035     Keldysh Institute of Applied Mathematics,
00036     see http://elizarova.imamod.ru/selection-of-papers.html
00037 
00038     A comprehensive description of QGD equations and their applications
00039     can be found here:
00040     \verbatim
00041     Elizarova, T.G.
00042     "Quasi-Gas Dynamic equations"
00043     Springer, 2009
00044     \endverbatim
00045 
00046     A brief of theory on QGD and QHD system of equations:
00047     \verbatim
00048     Elizarova, T.G. and Sheretov, Y.V.
00049     "Theoretical and numerical analysis of quasi-gasdynamic and quasi-hydrodynamic
00050     equations"
00051     J. Computational Mathematics and Mathematical Physics, vol. 41, no. 2, pp 219-234,

00053     \endverbatim
